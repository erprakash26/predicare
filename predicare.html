The Problem

Families struggle with reactive eldercare.

Pain points

Why current systems fall short

  • • Monitoring aging loved ones living alone is stressful and inconsistent.
  • • Most solutions react after an incident like a fall or health crisis.
  • • Falls, missed medications, and subtle health changes often go unnoticed.
  • • Families need reliable, daily insight, not occasional check-ins.
Our solution

PredictCare: proactive, AI-powered support

PredictCare delivers an advanced monitoring system that connects wearable and home sensors to predictive AI models. Instead of waiting for something to go wrong, we detect anomalies early and forecast risks — so caregivers can act in time.

  • ✓ Monitor continuous data from wristband and home sensors.
  • ✓ Predict patterns and future risk using AI insights.
  • ✓ Alert caregivers instantly for proactive intervention.
